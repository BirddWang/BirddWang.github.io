About

I am a Research Assistant at the Artificial Intelligence Research Center, Chang Gung University. My research centers on Knowledge Distillation — investigating its core concepts and applying them across multiple projects. My work spans Natural Language Processing and the medical image domain. More broadly, I aim to use Knowledge Distillation as a lens to better understand and interpret the inner workings of AI models.
